SOFTWARE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Software Services Agreement is entered into between Alpha Manufacturing Co. ("Client") and Nova Systems Ltd. ("Vendor").

1. Services
Vendor will implement and maintain a workflow automation platform for Client, including integration support, quarterly reporting, and basic user training.

2. Fees and Payment
Client will pay Vendor a one-time implementation fee of USD 25,000 and a recurring service fee of USD 8,000 per month.
Invoices are due within 15 days after receipt.
The agreement does not describe any late-payment interest, suspension rights, or dispute procedure for challenged invoices.

3. Term
The initial term is 24 months and renews automatically for successive 12-month periods unless either party gives 60 days' written notice before renewal.

4. Data Protection
Vendor will process Client data as necessary to provide the services and will use commercially reasonable safeguards.
The agreement does not define data retention periods, subcontractor approval requirements, cross-border transfer controls, or breach notification timelines.

5. Confidentiality
Each party must keep the other party's confidential information secret for 3 years after disclosure.
There is no express carve-out for information independently developed or required to be disclosed by law.

6. Intellectual Property
Vendor retains ownership of its pre-existing materials and general know-how.
The agreement is unclear on ownership of custom deliverables, training materials, and workflow templates created during the project.

7. Warranties
Vendor warrants that it will perform services in a professional manner.
No service levels, uptime commitments, response times, or warranty remedies are stated.

8. Liability
Vendor's total liability is limited to the fees paid in the 3 months preceding the claim.
The agreement does not address exclusions for gross negligence, willful misconduct, confidentiality breaches, or data protection violations.

9. Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured within 30 days after written notice.
Client does not have a clear termination right for repeated service failures or prolonged security incidents.

10. Governing Law
This agreement is governed by the laws of California.
